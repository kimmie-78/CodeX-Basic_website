Damesfontein

Fact 1: This restored 19th-century farmhouse offers a glimpse into the lives of early Karoo settlers. Fact 2: The farmhouse belonged to the Steyn family for five generations, and the collection of artifacts within showcases their history and farming practices. Fact 3: Visitors can tour the well-preserved rooms, explore the farmyard with traditional outbuildings, and learn about the region's agricultural heritage.

Owl House

Fact 1: Created by eccentric artist Helen Martins, The Owl House is a whimsical wonderland adorned with sculptures and mosaics crafted from everyday objects like ostrich eggshells. Fact 1: Created by eccentric artist Helen Martins, The Owl House is a whimsical wonderland adorned with sculptures and mosaics crafted from everyday objects like ostrich eggshells. Fact 2: Martins believed she was guided by a "little owl" spirit, inspiring the house's name and owl motif. Fact 3: Today, The Owl House is a national monument, preserving Martins' unique artistic vision for future generations.
